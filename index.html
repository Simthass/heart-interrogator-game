THE HEART INTERROGATOR

A psychological puzzle game where you must decide: trust the AI or trust yourself? Every choice matters.

GAME FEATURES

AI DECEPTION

The AI suspect might lie or tell the truth. You never know! It uses real API to decide randomly.

50/50 Chance

RISK VS REWARD

Trust the AI: +20 points but if it lies you lose life. Verify yourself: +10 points but safe.

Strategic Choice

TIME PRESSURE

1

COUNT HEARTS

Look at the puzzle image. Count how many hearts you see.

2

LISTEN TO AI

The AI suspect gives an answer. It might be lying!

3

MAKE CHOICE

Trust AI (+20) or Verify yourself (+10). Choose wisely!

4

SURVIVE

10 rounds. 3 lives. Wrong answers cost lives. Highest score wins!
